Our Faith

 

Since Saint John the Evangelist School is a Catholic school, the teachings of the Catholic Church are an integral part of the day in every classroom, serving as the foundation of life on campus. Students prepare for First Holy Communion, Reconciliation and Confirmation. They also help plan weekly liturgies. While students of all faiths are welcome, we ask all our students to subscribe to the basic tenets of respect for self and others.
