Welcome to St. John the Evangelist Catholic School, a Transitional Kindergarten through Eighth Grade coeducational Catholic school located in Carmichael, California. We are a ministry of St. John the Evangelist Parish and are a part of the Diocese of Sacramento.

Throughout these pages, you will find information about our full-day Kindergarten program, and our excellent spiritual, academic, and enrichment opportunities available to our students. We are fully accredited by the Western Catholic Educational Association (WCEA) and the Western Association of Schools and Colleges (WASC).

Academic excellence is the hallmark of an SJE education. The students are challenged to meet the highest academic standards, built on the foundation of Christ’s love. In addition to a challenging curriculum that is aligned with Diocesan and state standards, we offer a wide enrichment program which includes art, music, drama, technology, Spanish, and athletics.

We take pride in the fact that our mission calls us to educate the whole child. In partnership with the parents, we strive to prepare our students to become loving and productive members of society. Through the curriculum and many co-curricular activities at our school, we seek to open eyes and doors to the wonderful possibilities for the future.
