Hot Lunch Program

St. John the Evangelist School offers a hot lunch program every full day of school. A nutritious hot meal, including salad bar and/or fresh fruit, is prepared on-site and served to the students. A lunch calendar is sent home monthly and orders are placed and paid for ahead of time. All students who attend the Extension Program must bring a lunch from home on minimum days.

Milk and juice are available for purchase separately.

Students who do not order hot lunch must bring lunches from home. We encourage parents to make certain that these lunches are healthy.
